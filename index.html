Plast Brown

PLAST BROWN is the concentrated mixture of high-quality brown pigment, the polymer carrier, and other additives. It is widely applicable in a wide range plastic industries for coloring polymer at affordable cost and good dispersion at all times.

In addition, superior concentration mixture of additives + pigments makes the perfect selections of coloring polymers at lowest LDR at economical proposition.

With the suitability for various processes involving polymers such as PE, PP , it plays a vital role as one of the most primary colors that comprises of high quality pigments in different proportions as per the color tone/intensity required by the customers.

Hence, it adds value and aesthetics for Client’s finished products’ color, the blending could produce the most vivid color customized as your application specific inquiries.

It improves the features of end plastics such as durability against extreme weather conditions, heat resistance and eco-friendly with environment.

All our products are Food grade , ROHS compliant .

WE at BAJAJ PLAST make many color masterbatches created through the combination of the three principal color elements known as 3 chromatic properties, namely

  • HUE ( Specific colour name )
  • VALUE ( Amount of light in colour – Brighter / Darker)
  • CHROMA (Degree of colour – Stronger / milder )
